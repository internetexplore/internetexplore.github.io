Bizarre Ride II The Pharcyde (1992)

The Pharcyde

Passin' Me By

The Pharcyde lives up to their title on this album. This west coast group broke out on the scene with their "bizarre" assortment of obscure beats and goofy lyricism. This LP is full of personality and originality while still delivering an accessible and cohesive project. My 3 favorite song from the album are Passin' Me By, Pack The Pipe and Soul Flour.
