The Infamous (1995)

Mobb Deep

Shook Ones

This sophmore album from the rap/production duo Mobb Deep is a dark and gritty take on east coast hiphop. This Albums grimey lyricism mixed with some immaculate samples on the beats create a vivid picture of Queensbridge, New york in '95. My 3 favorite songs are Shook Ones, PT. 2, The Start of Your Ending and Cradle to the Grave
